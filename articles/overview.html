Collaborative Telepresence: An Overview

Telepresence is the ability for humans to interact remotely, with the aid of technology, to simulate physically being in an area. This concept has the potential to enhance many areas fields and industries by allowing people to physically transfer their skills across geographical boundaries and reduce the risks or implications of having humans work on dangerous sites.

The technology has the potential to revolutionize these industries and increasing inclusion by allowing persons previously unable, the chance to participate and share their input. This is where the collaborative part comes in.

By enhancing the ability to work remotely less opportunities can be missed as well as the quality of interaction can be increased to a point where it is on par with if the person were physically there.
