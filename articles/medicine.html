Remote Treatment

Using telepresence patients can be provided treatment that they could not have access to otherwise, remotely. A specialist for a difficult surgery can work remotely all over the world using a telepresence robot to carry out the delicate surgical proceedure using his knowledge and skills

Telepresence can be used on the other end by having patients connected to specialized sensory devices that can monitor their health status. This can alert a doctor or other emergency personel to the condition of a patient such as an elderly person living on their own

Militaries have explored using this to monitor their solders so military medics can find injured solders much easier on the battle field.
